=== LEAVE POLICY ===
Annual Leave:
- All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year.
- Leave must be applied at least 3 days in advance through the HR portal.
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days to the next year.
- Leave encashment is allowed for up to 5 days at the end of the financial year.

Sick Leave:
- Employees are entitled to 12 days of sick leave per year.
- Sick leave beyond 3 consecutive days requires a medical certificate.
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward or encashed.

Maternity Leave:
- Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Must be applied at least 4 weeks before the expected date of delivery.
- Additional 4 weeks unpaid leave can be requested.

Paternity Leave:
- Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave.
- Must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth.

Emergency Leave:
- Employees can apply for up to 3 days of emergency leave per year.
- Emergency leave is for immediate family emergencies only.
- Must be notified to manager within 2 hours of absence.

=== WORK FROM HOME POLICY ===
- Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week.
- WFH must be approved by the direct manager at least 1 day in advance.
- Employees must be available on all communication channels during WFH.
- WFH is not applicable for employees in their probation period (first 3 months).
- Internet allowance of INR 500 per month is provided for WFH employees.

=== SALARY AND PAYROLL POLICY ===
- Salaries are credited on the last working day of every month.
- Salary slips are available on the HR portal by the 2nd of the following month.
- Annual appraisals are conducted in April every year.
- Performance bonuses are paid in May after appraisal completion.
- Tax deductions follow the Indian Income Tax Act and are updated annually.
- Reimbursement claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense.

=== ONBOARDING POLICY ===
- New employees must complete onboarding within the first 2 weeks.
- Documents required: Offer letter, ID proof, Address proof, Educational certificates, PAN card.
- Laptop and system access are provided on Day 1.
- A buddy is assigned to every new employee for the first 30 days.
- Probation period is 3 months for all employees.

=== RESIGNATION AND EXIT POLICY ===
- Notice period is 60 days for all employees.
- Employees must submit resignation through the HR portal.
- Final settlement including PF, gratuity, and pending salary is processed within 45 days.
- Exit interview is mandatory before the last working day.
- Company assets must be returned before the full and final settlement.
- Non-disclosure agreement must be signed during offboarding.

=== PERFORMANCE REVIEW POLICY ===
- Performance reviews are conducted twice a year: April and October.
- Employees are rated on a 5-point scale: Outstanding, Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory.
- Ratings below Meets Expectations for 2 consecutive reviews may result in a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).
- Employees can raise performance disputes within 7 days of receiving the review.

=== TRAVEL AND EXPENSE POLICY ===
- Business travel must be pre-approved by the department head.
- Domestic travel: Economy class for flights, AC 3-tier for trains.
- International travel: Business class for flights above 6 hours.
- Hotel allowance: INR 5000 per night for domestic, USD 150 per night for international.
- All expense claims must be submitted with original receipts within 30 days.
- Per diem for meals: INR 800 per day domestic, USD 50 per day international.

=== HEALTH AND BENEFITS POLICY ===
- All employees and their immediate family are covered under group health insurance.
- Insurance coverage: INR 5 lakhs per family per year.
- Pre-existing conditions are covered after 1 year of employment.
- Term life insurance of INR 50 lakhs is provided at no cost.
- Annual health checkup is provided free of cost for employees above 35 years.
- Gym reimbursement of INR 1000 per month is available upon submission of receipts.

=== ANTI-HARASSMENT POLICY ===
- The company has zero tolerance for workplace harassment of any kind.
- Employees can report harassment to the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC).
- All complaints are investigated within 30 days.
- Retaliation against complainants is strictly prohibited.
- Anonymous reporting is available through the HR portal.

=== TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT POLICY ===
- Each employee is entitled to INR 25,000 per year for training and certifications.
- Training budget must be pre-approved by the department head.
- Employees must share key learnings with the team after external trainings.
- Internal training programs are free and available on the LMS portal.
- Sponsored certifications require the employee to stay for at least 1 year post-completion.
